Japan’s Geographical Location

Japan is located in East Asia, in the western Pacific Ocean. It lies east of the Sea of Japan, the Korean Peninsula, and China, and west of the Pacific Ocean. Because of its geography, Japan experiences varied climates across the country. In winter, the regions facing the Sea of Japan receive heavy snowfall, while the Pacific side often has dry and sunny weather known as “winter sunshine.”
